Wheelchair Facilities

Belleek woods has great accessibility for wheelchair users and allows them to explore the wood in a very intimate way


Wheelchair Accesible Parking

With easily accesible, wheelchair designated parking, the woods are very much open to all abilities. The parking facilities are centrally located next to the duck pond. From this access point visitors can travel into the heart of the woods and have easy access to the riverside. There are trails leading in both directions along the riverfront with breathtaking views and there are also paths through the wood for a wonderful intimate experience of what the woods has to offer.

Closed Surface Trails

With a good selection of closed surface trails, all with managable gradients. Wheelchair users will be able to enjoy and explore all the woods has to offer. The trails follow the riverside and run the whole length of the wood from Ballina Town football pitch to the car park on the Old French Road. This provides in excess of 5km of trails to be enjoyed. There are also wheelchair friendly picnic benches located with wonderful views of the Crete Boom and down the river estuary.
